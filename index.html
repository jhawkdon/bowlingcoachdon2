About Me

Hi I'm Coach Don! My love for bowling started at the age of 5, and I've been hooked ever since. I've bowled in leagues and tournaments all over the country, including at the collegiate level. Along the way, I've made many lifelong friends and developed a deep appreciation for the sport.,

After years of helping others with their game, I decided to become a USBC Bronze Level coach. Since then, I've found my time coaching to be incredibly rewarding. I love meeting new people with a desire to improve their game. Seeing the progress my students make and the joy they experience when they achieve their goals is what drives me.
